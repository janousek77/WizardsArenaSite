5/28/2019

5/30/2019
- Added two sprite sheets for a new golem enemy, containing its walking and spawning animations. Created By Ben

- Imported in mage, ghost, zombie, skeleton, three-headed wolf, wraith, vampire, tree ent, goblin and wraith sprite sheets. All taken from RPG Maker VX Ace. On their site they state that games can be sold using their assets so we figured we'd just utilize them for our own game using unity.

- Made prefabs for ghost, zombie, skeleton, and three-headed wolf to be used as our initial monsters to be spawned at random.

- Created and coded in animations for wizard sprite.

- Added in music for our main forest scene. Music is from: Fantasy Battle Music Free Pack from the unity asset store.



5/31/2019
- Imported a potion sprite sheet found here: https://opengameart.org/content/shiny-rpg-potions-16x16 created by Bonsaiheldin | http://bonsaiheld.org - Created a potion controller script that contols the effects of each of the four potions: Healing character 25 health points, Increasing the fire rate of spells for 5 seconds, Increasing the characters speed for 5 seconds, Doubling the damage done by spells for 5 seconds. All potions stay on the screen for 10 seconds - Created and coded in animations for our four spawnable monsters. - Added in some code that causes potions to flash for three seconds before disappearing



6/1/2019
- There are now 5 waves of enemies, each slightly faster than the last. Potions will also flash for three seconds before disappearing. Finally, the enemy animations were made smoother.

- Damage inflicted on the player or on the enemies have pop-up text indicators, as do small messages for each potion the player picks up to let them know what it did.
