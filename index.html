5/31/2019
- Implemented potions that serve as power-ups as a rare (10%) drop from defeated enemies. Their effects include increased damage, increased speed, decreased cooldowns for attacks, and healing up to 25 points of damage.


6/1/2019

- Damage inflicted on the player or on the enemies have pop-up text indicators, as do small messages for each potion the player picks up to let them know what it did.
